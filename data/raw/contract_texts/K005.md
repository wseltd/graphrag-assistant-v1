**Effective Date:** 1 August 2025

**Parties:**

This Infrastructure Monitoring and Licence Assurance Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 1 August 2025 by and between:

**Thornbridge Consulting Ltd**, a company incorporated in England and Wales with registration number 13047285, whose registered office is at 89 Broad Street, Bristol, BS1 2EP (hereinafter "the Client"); and

**Vantage Systems Ltd**, a company incorporated in England and Wales with registration number 08163924, whose registered office is at 23 Commerce Street, Edinburgh, EH1 1YZ (hereinafter "the Supplier").

## Background

Thornbridge Consulting Ltd operates a distributed consultancy practice across multiple UK office locations and depends on the continuous availability of its server and network infrastructure to support active client engagements. Following a period of unplanned downtime in early 2025 that interrupted two concurrent client projects, the Client commissioned an internal technology review to identify gaps in its monitoring and incident detection capabilities.

That review recommended a dedicated infrastructure monitoring platform providing real-time alerting, capacity trend analysis, centralised log aggregation, and health dashboards from a single management console, coupled with an annual assurance programme to keep the platform current and backed by priority technical support.

Following a market assessment of six candidate platforms evaluated on functionality, integration capability, and five-year total cost of ownership, the Client selected the Supplier on the basis of the Infrastructure Monitoring Suite's enterprise deployment record, its compatibility with the Client's existing virtualisation environment, and the Supplier's UK support coverage.

## Scope of Supply

The Supplier shall supply and licence the Infrastructure Monitoring Suite to Thornbridge Consulting Ltd under the terms of this Agreement. The licence is perpetual, non-exclusive, and non-transferable, entitling the Client to deploy the platform across all UK office sites for the duration of this Agreement.

The licence covers monitoring of up to one hundred and fifty (150) managed devices, encompassing physical servers, virtual machines, network switches, and firewalls across all in-scope sites. The platform provides continuous availability monitoring with configurable alerting thresholds, capacity utilisation trending, and a web-based management dashboard accessible to up to twenty (20) named administrator accounts. The Supplier shall perform remote installation validation within ten (10) business days of the Effective Date.

In addition to the software licence, the Supplier shall deliver the Software Licence Assurance Programme for the initial contract year and each renewal year while this Agreement remains in force. The programme entitles the Client to all major and minor product version releases, access to the Supplier's technical support portal, and priority escalation for critical platform defects.

The Supplier's nominated contract lead is Rajesh Patel, Technical Director at Vantage Systems Ltd. Rajesh Patel is responsible for licence activation, deployment support, and technical escalation throughout the term. The Client's nominated contract manager is Emma Thornton, Principal Consultant at Thornbridge Consulting Ltd. Emma Thornton holds authority to approve configuration changes, certify deliverables, and authorise assurance renewals on behalf of the Client.

## Payment Terms

The total licence fee for the Infrastructure Monitoring Suite is twenty-two thousand pounds sterling (£22,000.00), exclusive of VAT, payable as a single lump sum within thirty (30) calendar days of the Effective Date.

The Software Licence Assurance Programme shall be charged annually at nine thousand five hundred pounds sterling (£9,500.00) per year, exclusive of VAT. The first annual assurance fee is due within thirty (30) calendar days of the Effective Date. Subsequent fees shall be invoiced sixty (60) calendar days before each anniversary and payable within thirty (30) calendar days of invoice receipt.

All invoices shall reference contract number K005 and be addressed to the accounts payable function of Thornbridge Consulting Ltd. Payment shall be made by BACS transfer to the bank account notified by Vantage Systems Ltd in writing. Any invoice not disputed within ten (10) business days of receipt shall be deemed accepted. Overdue amounts shall accrue interest at three per cent (3%) per annum above the Bank of England base rate, compounding monthly.

## Service Levels

During the term of the Software Licence Assurance Programme, the Supplier shall make its technical support portal available Monday to Friday, 08:00 to 18:00 UK time, excluding public holidays. For Priority 1 defects — complete loss of monitoring across five or more devices or alerting engine failure — the Supplier shall acknowledge within two (2) business hours and resolve within one (1) business day. For Priority 2 defects — material degradation of monitoring coverage or dashboard unavailability — the Supplier shall acknowledge within four (4) business hours and resolve within three (3) business days.

Rajesh Patel shall chair a quarterly review with Emma Thornton covering platform performance, upcoming releases, and open support items. Annual assurance renewal confirmation shall be provided in writing sixty (60) calendar days before the end of each assurance year.

## Confidentiality

Each Party acknowledges that in performing its obligations it may receive Confidential Information belonging to the other. Confidential Information includes system architecture details, security configurations, network topology, managed device inventories, pricing schedules, and proprietary software documentation. Neither Party shall disclose such information to any third party without prior written consent, except where required by law or court order. This obligation shall survive termination or expiry for five (5) years.

## Liability

The Supplier's aggregate liability to the Client for all claims arising under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Thornbridge Consulting Ltd to Vantage Systems Ltd in the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim. Neither Party shall be liable for indirect or consequential loss, loss of revenue, loss of data, or loss of business opportunity. Nothing in this Agreement limits either Party's liability for fraud, death, or personal injury caused by negligence.

## Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving sixty (60) calendar days' written notice to the other at any time after the first anniversary of the Effective Date. Either Party may terminate immediately if the other commits a material breach and, where capable of remedy, fails to remedy it within thirty (30) calendar days of written notice. Thornbridge Consulting Ltd may terminate immediately if Vantage Systems Ltd enters insolvency proceedings or ceases to carry on business.

Upon termination, the Client shall cease using the licensed software and, at the Supplier's election, return or securely destroy all copies of the Infrastructure Monitoring Suite, providing written certification of destruction within ten (10) business days. Any prepaid assurance fees attributable to periods after termination shall be refunded to Thornbridge Consulting Ltd on a pro-rata basis within thirty (30) calendar days.
