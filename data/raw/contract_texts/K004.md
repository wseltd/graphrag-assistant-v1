# Managed IT Services Agreement — Contract K004

**Effective Date:** 1 July 2025

**Parties:**

This Managed IT Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 1 July 2025 by and between:

**Meridian Holdings Ltd**, a company incorporated in England and Wales with registration number 12847391, whose registered office is at 45 King Street, London, EC2V 8RF (hereinafter "the Client"); and

**CentroLogic Industrie SA**, a company incorporated in France with registration number 75018324, whose registered office is at 15 Rue de la Paix, Paris, 75001, France (hereinafter "the Supplier").

Together the Client and the Supplier are referred to as "the Parties."

---

## Background

Meridian Holdings Ltd operates a portfolio of UK business units that rely on continuous availability of on-premises server infrastructure, network systems, and licensed software applications. Following the completion of a cloud migration programme, the Client identified a requirement for structured managed services to maintain the retained on-premises estate and ensure all software licences remain current, compliant, and supported.

Following a competitive procurement exercise that included technical evaluation and a panel review, Meridian Holdings Ltd selected CentroLogic Industrie SA on the basis of the Supplier's pan-European managed services capability, its demonstrable experience in multi-site infrastructure support, and its established framework for licence compliance management across enterprise environments.

## Scope of Services

CentroLogic Industrie SA shall provide the Managed IT Services Package to Meridian Holdings Ltd under the terms of this Agreement. The Managed IT Services Package encompasses twenty-four-hour monitoring of the Client's on-premises server estate, proactive alerting for hardware and software failures, remote and on-site break-fix support, operating system patch management, configuration baseline management, and monthly infrastructure health reporting.

In addition to managed infrastructure services, the Supplier shall deliver the Annual Maintenance & Support Contract covering all licensed software applications deployed across Meridian Holdings Ltd's UK business units. The Annual Maintenance & Support Contract includes version update management, licence compliance tracking, vendor escalation on behalf of the Client, software asset register maintenance, and quarterly software asset review meetings.

The Supplier's nominated account manager for this engagement is Claire Fontaine, Director of Operations at CentroLogic Industrie SA. Claire Fontaine is responsible for service delivery oversight, monthly performance reporting, escalation management, and contract compliance throughout the term. The Client's nominated contract manager is James Blackwood, Chief Procurement Officer of Meridian Holdings Ltd. James Blackwood holds authority to approve service change requests, raise formal service escalations, and certify service performance on behalf of the Client.

## Service Levels

The Supplier shall maintain the following minimum service levels for all services delivered under this Agreement. For Priority 1 incidents, defined as complete loss of availability of a production server, network segment, or business-critical application, the Supplier shall acknowledge the incident within fifteen (15) minutes of detection or notification and achieve full service restoration within four (4) hours. For Priority 2 incidents, defined as significant degradation of a production system without complete loss of service, the Supplier shall acknowledge within one (1) hour and restore normal service within eight (8) business hours.

Planned maintenance windows shall be scheduled on Sunday mornings between 02:00 and 06:00 local time. The Supplier shall provide at least five (5) business days' notice of any planned maintenance activity, except where emergency security patching is required to address a critical vulnerability, in which case forty-eight (48) hours' notice shall suffice. Monthly service reports shall be delivered to the Client's nominated contact within five (5) business days of the end of each calendar month. Failure to meet Priority 1 restoration targets in any two consecutive months shall entitle the Client to claim a service credit equal to ten per cent (10%) of the monthly managed services fee for each affected month.

## Payment Terms

The Managed IT Services Package shall be charged at a monthly rate of three thousand six hundred pounds sterling (£3,600.00) exclusive of VAT, payable monthly in arrears. The Annual Maintenance & Support Contract shall be charged at eighteen thousand pounds sterling (£18,000.00) per contract year exclusive of VAT, invoiced annually in advance on the anniversary of the Effective Date.

All invoices shall reference contract number K004 and be addressed to the accounts payable function of Meridian Holdings Ltd. Payment is due within thirty (30) calendar days of receipt of a valid invoice. Any invoice not formally disputed within ten (10) business days of receipt shall be deemed accepted. Overdue amounts shall accrue interest at three per cent (3%) per annum above the Bank of England base rate, compounding monthly.

## Confidentiality

Each Party shall treat as confidential all information received from the other in connection with this Agreement. Confidential information includes, without limitation, the Client's infrastructure topology, security policies, software asset lists, operational data, and business plans, as well as the Supplier's service methodologies, toolsets, pricing structures, and client references. Neither Party shall disclose such information to any third party without prior written consent, except where required by applicable law or court order. This obligation shall continue for five (5) years following termination or expiry of this Agreement.

## Liability

The Supplier's aggregate liability to the Client for any claims arising under or in connection with this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Meridian Holdings Ltd to CentroLogic Industrie SA in the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim. Neither Party shall be liable to the other for indirect or consequential loss, loss of revenue, loss of data, or loss of business opportunity. Nothing in this Agreement limits either Party's liability for fraud, death, or personal injury caused by negligence, or for any other liability that cannot lawfully be excluded.

## Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving ninety (90) calendar days' written notice to the other at any time after the first anniversary of the Effective Date. Either Party may terminate immediately on written notice if the other commits a material breach and, where capable of remedy, fails to remedy it within thirty (30) calendar days of written notice. Meridian Holdings Ltd may terminate with immediate effect if CentroLogic Industrie SA enters insolvency proceedings, ceases to carry on business, or is unable to provide adequately qualified personnel to fulfil its obligations.

Upon termination for any reason, the Supplier shall complete all outstanding monthly and quarterly reports, transfer monitoring access credentials to the Client, and hand over the current software asset register within ten (10) business days. Any fees paid in advance for services not yet delivered shall be refunded to Meridian Holdings Ltd within thirty (30) calendar days of the termination date.
