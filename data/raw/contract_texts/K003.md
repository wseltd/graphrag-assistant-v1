# Hardware Supply Agreement — Contract K003

**Effective Date:** 1 June 2025

**Parties:**

This Hardware Supply Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 1 June 2025 by and between:

**Meridian Holdings Ltd**, a company incorporated in England and Wales with registration number 12847391, whose registered office is at 45 King Street, London, EC2V 8RF (hereinafter "the Buyer"); and

**Albrecht und Partner GmbH**, a company incorporated in Germany with registration number 30482719, whose registered office is at Unter den Linden 47, Berlin, 10117, Germany (hereinafter "the Supplier").

Together the Buyer and the Supplier are referred to as "the Parties."

---

## Background

Meridian Holdings Ltd has determined that its UK data centre and office network infrastructure requires a programme of hardware refresh and security uplift. The existing network perimeter equipment has reached end-of-support status, and a recent internal security audit identified gaps in both physical rack capacity and perimeter defence coverage across the Buyer's London and regional facilities.

Following a structured procurement exercise that included technical evaluation and a competitive price comparison, Meridian Holdings Ltd selected Albrecht und Partner GmbH as its preferred hardware supplier on the basis of product certification, delivery lead times, and post-installation warranty terms. The Supplier has confirmed that both the Network Security Appliance and the Server Rack Unit 42U are available from its standard production stock and can be delivered within the timelines set out in this Agreement.

## Scope of Supply

Albrecht und Partner GmbH shall supply and deliver the following hardware items to Meridian Holdings Ltd under the terms of this Agreement:

- **Network Security Appliance** — twelve (12) units at a unit price of twelve thousand five hundred pounds sterling (£12,500.00), giving a line total of one hundred and fifty thousand pounds sterling (£150,000.00) exclusive of applicable taxes.
- **Server Rack Unit 42U** — eight (8) units at a unit price of three thousand two hundred pounds sterling (£3,200.00), giving a line total of twenty-five thousand six hundred pounds sterling (£25,600.00) exclusive of applicable taxes.

The aggregate hardware value under this Agreement is one hundred and seventy-five thousand six hundred pounds sterling (£175,600.00) exclusive of VAT. All hardware shall be delivered to the Buyer's primary data centre at 45 King Street, London, EC2V 8RF, unless the Buyer designates an alternative delivery address in writing at least ten (10) business days before the scheduled delivery date.

The Supplier's nominated contract lead for this Agreement is Heinrich Müller, Managing Director of Albrecht und Partner GmbH. Heinrich Müller holds authority to confirm delivery schedules, approve substitutions, and execute change orders on behalf of the Supplier. The Buyer's nominated contract manager is James Blackwood, Chief Procurement Officer of Meridian Holdings Ltd. James Blackwood holds authority to accept deliveries, approve invoices, and authorise any changes to the agreed specification in writing.

All hardware must be delivered with full manufacturer documentation, tested and certified to CE and UKCA marking requirements as applicable. The Supplier shall provide installation verification for each Network Security Appliance and rack assembly guidance for each Server Rack Unit 42U at no additional charge. Legal matters relating to this Agreement are handled on behalf of the Supplier by Katarina Bauer, Legal Counsel at Albrecht und Partner GmbH.

## Payment Terms

The total contract value of one hundred and seventy-five thousand six hundred pounds sterling (£175,600.00) exclusive of VAT shall be invoiced and paid in two stages. The first invoice, covering fifty per cent (£87,800.00), shall be raised by Albrecht und Partner GmbH on the date of despatch confirmation and is due within thirty (30) calendar days of receipt by Meridian Holdings Ltd. The second invoice, covering the remaining fifty per cent (£87,800.00), shall be raised following written acceptance of the full delivery by James Blackwood and is due within thirty (30) calendar days of that invoice date.

All invoices must reference contract number K003 and be addressed to the accounts payable function of Meridian Holdings Ltd. Payment shall be made by BACS or international bank transfer to the account notified by Albrecht und Partner GmbH in writing. Overdue amounts shall accrue interest at three per cent (3%) per annum above the European Central Bank base rate, compounding monthly. Any invoice not formally disputed within ten (10) business days of receipt shall be deemed accepted.

## Liability

The Supplier's total aggregate liability to the Buyer under or in connection with this Agreement, whether in contract, tort, or otherwise, shall not exceed the total fees paid by Meridian Holdings Ltd to Albrecht und Partner GmbH under this Agreement. Neither Party shall be liable to the other for indirect or consequential losses, including loss of revenue, loss of data, or loss of business opportunity. Nothing in this Agreement limits either Party's liability for fraud, death, or personal injury caused by negligence, or for product liability claims arising under applicable statute.

## Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving thirty (30) calendar days' written notice to the other if no delivery has yet commenced. Once delivery of any item has commenced, termination shall not affect the Buyer's obligation to pay for hardware already despatched. Either Party may terminate immediately on written notice if the other commits a material breach and, where capable of remedy, fails to remedy that breach within twenty-one (21) calendar days of receiving written notice specifying the breach in reasonable detail. Meridian Holdings Ltd may terminate with immediate effect if Albrecht und Partner GmbH enters insolvency proceedings or ceases to trade.

## Governing Law

This Agreement, and any dispute or claim arising out of or in connection with it, shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales. Each Party irrevocably submits to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.
