# Cloud Migration Consultancy Agreement — Contract K002

**Effective Date:** 15 May 2025

**Parties:**

This Cloud Migration Consultancy Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 15 May 2025 by and between:

**Meridian Holdings Ltd**, a company incorporated in England and Wales with registration number 12847391, whose registered office is at 45 King Street, London, EC2V 8RF (hereinafter "the Client"); and

**Hartwell Solutions Ltd**, a company incorporated in England and Wales with registration number 11502847, whose registered office is at 78 High Street, Birmingham, B1 1BB (hereinafter "the Consultant").

Together the Client and the Consultant are referred to as "the Parties."

---

## Background

Meridian Holdings Ltd operates a portfolio of UK business units that rely on a mixture of on-premises server infrastructure and legacy virtualisation platforms established over a period of fifteen years. As part of a multi-year technology transformation programme, the Client has determined that migrating selected workloads to a private cloud environment will reduce operational expenditure, improve resilience, and create a more scalable foundation for future enterprise applications.

Following a structured market assessment and a competitive shortlisting exercise, the Client selected Hartwell Solutions Ltd on the basis of the Consultant's demonstrable experience in enterprise infrastructure migration, its Birmingham-based delivery team, and its methodology for phased workload transition with minimal disruption to live operations.

## Scope of Services

Hartwell Solutions Ltd shall provide Cloud Migration Consultancy services to Meridian Holdings Ltd under this Agreement. The engagement shall proceed in three phases: discovery and assessment, migration planning, and supervised migration execution.

During the discovery and assessment phase, the Consultant's team shall conduct a full audit of the Client's existing server estate, map application dependencies, identify migration blockers, and produce a written Infrastructure Assessment Report within four weeks of the Effective Date. During the migration planning phase, the Consultant shall develop a detailed workload migration schedule, define rollback procedures for each workload, and present the Migration Runbook to the Client for approval.

During the migration execution phase, the Consultant shall provide hands-on technical support for the migration of up to forty virtual machine workloads, including pre-migration testing, cutover coordination, and post-migration validation. The Consultant shall assign a minimum of one senior consultant to each migration window.

The Consultant's nominated project lead for this engagement is Oliver Hartley, Senior Consultant at Hartwell Solutions Ltd. Oliver Hartley is responsible for day-to-day delivery management, milestone sign-off, resource scheduling, and escalation of technical risks to the Client. The Client's contract manager is James Blackwood, Chief Procurement Officer of Meridian Holdings Ltd. James Blackwood holds authority to approve the Migration Runbook, authorise scope adjustments in writing, and certify the completion of each phase on behalf of the Client.

## Payment Terms

The Cloud Migration Consultancy services shall be charged at a daily rate of one thousand two hundred and fifty pounds sterling (£1,250.00 per day), exclusive of VAT. The total contracted volume is eighty consultant days, giving a maximum contract value of one hundred thousand pounds sterling (£100,000.00) exclusive of VAT.

Invoices shall be raised by Hartwell Solutions Ltd at the end of each calendar month, covering confirmed consultant days delivered in that month. Each invoice must quote contract reference K002 and be addressed to the accounts payable function of Meridian Holdings Ltd. Payment is due within thirty (30) calendar days of receipt of a valid invoice. Overdue amounts shall accrue interest at three per cent (3%) per annum above the Bank of England base rate. Any invoice not formally disputed within ten (10) business days of receipt shall be deemed accepted.

The Consultant shall maintain time records for all consultant days delivered under this Agreement and shall make those records available to the Client upon request within five business days.

## Intellectual Property

All deliverables produced solely for the Client under this Agreement, including the Infrastructure Assessment Report, the Migration Runbook, and any bespoke configuration scripts or documentation, shall vest in Meridian Holdings Ltd upon full payment of the associated fees. Pre-existing intellectual property belonging to Hartwell Solutions Ltd, including proprietary methodology frameworks, toolsets, and assessment templates used in the delivery of the services, shall remain the exclusive property of the Consultant. The Client is granted a non-exclusive, royalty-free licence to use such pre-existing materials solely to the extent necessary to maintain and operate the migrated infrastructure following completion of the engagement.

## Confidentiality

Each Party shall keep confidential all information received from the other Party that is designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would recognise as confidential given its nature and the circumstances of disclosure. Confidential information under this Agreement includes, without limitation, the Client's infrastructure topology, security configurations, vendor contracts, business plans, and financial data, as well as the Consultant's proprietary methodologies, pricing structures, and client references. Neither Party shall disclose such information to any third party without prior written consent, except where required by law or court order. This obligation shall continue for five (5) years following the termination or expiry of this Agreement.

## Liability

The Consultant's aggregate liability to the Client for any and all claims arising under or in connection with this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Meridian Holdings Ltd to Hartwell Solutions Ltd in the six (6) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim. Neither Party shall be liable for indirect or consequential losses, loss of data, loss of revenue, or loss of anticipated savings. Nothing in this Agreement limits either Party's liability for fraud, death, or personal injury caused by negligence, or for any other liability that cannot be excluded by law.

## Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement on thirty (30) calendar days' written notice to the other at any time after completion of the discovery and assessment phase. Either Party may terminate immediately on written notice if the other commits a material breach and, where capable of remedy, fails to remedy that breach within twenty-one (21) calendar days of written notice. Meridian Holdings Ltd may terminate with immediate effect if Hartwell Solutions Ltd enters insolvency proceedings, ceases to carry on business, or is unable to provide adequately qualified personnel to fulfil its obligations.

Upon termination, the Consultant shall deliver all completed and work-in-progress deliverables to the Client within ten (10) business days. Fees for consultant days confirmed as delivered prior to the termination date shall remain payable. Any fees paid in advance for services not yet delivered shall be refunded to Meridian Holdings Ltd within thirty (30) calendar days of the termination date.
